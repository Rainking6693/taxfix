Pro Tips for Maximum Tax Savings

  • Keep detailed records: Maintain receipts, invoices, and documentation for all business expenses throughout the year.
  • Track mileage religiously: Use apps like MileIQ or keep a detailed mileage log - this is often the biggest deduction.
  • Separate business and personal: Use dedicated business bank accounts and credit cards to simplify tracking.
  • Document business purpose: Note the business reason for each expense to justify deductions during an audit.
  • Consider quarterly payments: Pay estimated taxes quarterly to avoid penalties and manage cash flow.
  • Save for taxes: Set aside 25-30% of income for taxes throughout the year.
  • Review regularly: Use this checklist quarterly to ensure you're not missing ongoing deductions.
  • Get professional help: Complex situations benefit from professional tax guidance to maximize savings legally.
