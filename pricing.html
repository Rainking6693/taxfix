Pricing FAQ

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can change your plan anytime. If you upgrade, you'll only pay the prorated difference. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Absolutely. The DIY Tools come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. For monthly plans, you can cancel anytime and we offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee on your first month. Want to try before you buy? Start with our free deduction finder or tax calculator to see the value we provide.

What's included in the Full Service plan?

Everything! Monthly bookkeeping, professional tax preparation, dedicated tax professional, unlimited consultations, audit protection, and concierge service. Pricing starts at $299/month based on complexity. See examples of our comprehensive analysis in our freelancer guide or rideshare optimization guide.

Do you offer discounts for annual payments?

Yes! Pay annually for Guided Support and save 2 months (effectively 10 months for the price of 12). Full Service clients get a 15% discount on annual payments.

What if I have questions about which plan to choose?

Book a free 15-minute consultation! We'll review your situation and recommend the best plan for your needs and budget. No pressure, just honest advice. You can also explore our platform-specific guides: Uber/Lyft, DoorDash, Freelancers, or Etsy sellers to see what's most relevant to your work.

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

No hidden fees! DIY Tools is a one-time $29 payment. Monthly plans are exactly as advertised. Full Service may have one-time setup fees for complex business structures, which we'll discuss upfront.
